-- Building Basic Angular 4 App ---

How an Angular app Starts?

 - Components
You build your entire application using components. 
Components allow you to split up your logic/styling. 
component is a class, a typescript class that angular instantiates.
Each component needs to have a template!


Decorators are typescript features. they have the @ sign in front.


App Modules bundle components of our app into packages.
